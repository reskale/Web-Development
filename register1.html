What do you mean by Bootstrap 4 registration form? In Bootstrap 4, registration form is a list of fields in which a user will input data and submit it. It is useful in every situation where a registration is necessary. For Example: Various companies use registration forms to sign up customers for services, or other programs. It is also used for various job applications. Various portals are available for applying for jobs such as indeed, LinkedIn etc. In Schools and colleges a registration form is used to register students for a course in which students can fill their contact information, academic history etc. Let's take various examples of registration forms in Bootstrap4.
